Smart TV Setup & Activation Guide

Easy Step-by-Step Instructions for First-Time Setup

Setup WiFi Account Apps Help

1. Unbox & Power On Your TV

Step 1: Carefully unbox your TV and place it on a stable surface.
Step 2: Connect the power cable and press the Power button.
Step 3: Insert batteries into the remote control.
Important: Make sure your TV is placed in a well-ventilated area.

Screenshot Placeholder: TV Welcome Screen

2. Connect to WiFi

Step 1: Select your language and region.
Step 2: Choose your WiFi network from the list.
Step 3: Enter your WiFi password carefully.
Success: Once connected, your TV will check for updates automatically.

Screenshot Placeholder: WiFi Setup Screen

3. Create or Sign In to Your Account

Step 1: Visit the setup website shown on your TV screen.
Step 2: Enter the activation code displayed on your TV.
Step 3: Sign in or create a new account.
Tip: Use a strong password to secure your account.

Screenshot Placeholder: Activation Code Screen

4. Install and Use Apps

Step 1: Open the App Store from the home screen.
Step 2: Browse or search for apps like YouTube, Netflix, etc.
Step 3: Click Install and wait for download.
Success: Your apps are ready to use!

Screenshot Placeholder: App Store Screen

5. Troubleshooting & Help

Issue: WiFi not connecting Solution: Restart router and try again.
Issue: Activation code not working Solution: Refresh the code and re-enter.
Issue: Apps not loading Solution: Check internet connection or restart TV.
Still need help? Contact support or visit the official help website.
