Smart TV Setup & Activation Guide

Easy Step-by-Step Instructions for First-Time Setup

Start Setup
Setup WiFi Account Apps Help

1. Unbox & Power On

Step 1: Unbox your Smart TV carefully.
Step 2: Connect power cable.
Step 3: Turn ON TV using remote.
Keep TV on a stable surface.

2. Connect to WiFi

Step 1: Select language.
Step 2: Choose WiFi network.
Step 3: Enter password.
WiFi connected successfully.

3. Activation & Account

Step 1: Open setup website.
Step 2: Enter activation code.
Step 3: Login or sign up.
Keep account details safe.

4. Install Apps

Step 1: Open App Store.
Step 2: Search apps like YouTube/Netflix.
Step 3: Click Install.
Apps installed successfully.

5. Troubleshooting

WiFi issue → Restart router.
Activation error → Refresh code.
Apps not loading → Restart TV.
Contact support if needed.
